A - Move left
D - Move Right
W - Jump
SPACE - Fire arrow

When the powerup is collected;
Hold SHIFT - Slow fall
Hold SHIFT + Press W = Super Jump

Tips;
Max health possible is 11
Red hearts will increase current health by 1
Gold hearts will increase current health by 1 and lives by 1
Spikes hurt a lot, but are not instant kill
You can still "fall" out of the world via the ceiling if you jump too high
You can't fall to death in the dragon room, he wants you for himself
The dragon has 3 unique attacks
An arrow can block a dragon's fireball
The dragon must be hit by 25 arrows to be slain
Dont touch the dragon! Even with the strength potion, his jaws are deadly

Cheats;
Pressing 1 - Go to the first level
Pressing 2 - Go to the second level
Pressing 3 - Go to the third level
Pressing 9 - Will give you both powerups
Pressing i - Invincibility(Falling will still kill you)
Pressing Z - Go to "win state" room
Enter - Restart game
Escape - Exit game
